Background

My name is Justin. I am from Brooklyn, New York. I attend NYU where I study Math and Computer Science and I expect to graduate Spring 2025. I interned at Google as a SWE in the summer of 2024.

My love for design started in middle school, when I did graphic design for clients on Roblox. In high school I was introduced to computer science, which I thought was my new passion. I stopped graphic design, but I learned how to code, including websites.

Around my second year of college, I started to care about my dressing style. This sparked a new passion in me and I questioned what I really wanted to do with my life. I started drawing and designing again.

Design feels natural and fun to me. I am passionate about visuals and the stories behind them. I am trained in software engineering, but I hope to design for a living.
