About Experience Creative

I made ads, logos, clothes, and thumbnails. In high school I stopped, but I learned how to code websites. In college, I started designing websites more. You can see the various designs I've made in the Creative tab. I also took up interest in fashion in college, as I enjoy styling myself, but I am considering fashion design next.

While writing this, I am a senior expected to graduate next semester with a Math degree, but I realize my true passion lies in design of one form or another. It won't be easy, but I want to design for a living because that's really what makes me happy.
